Hacela is a mobile application that enables users to create online chamas and add their friends or family. Hacela offers efficiency and convenience in handling chama process through a tap on your screen. Contributions are made through the application offering more transparency as all the information about the chama are available for each member to see. Personal financial informations are securely stored ensuring unauthorized person is unable to access. Through Hacela, one can easily access loans from the chama you ae apart of at lower interest rates. Disbursement of money to members is also automated making fast and reliable. All the report about the chama will be available in mini statement PDF to ensure the members are able to follow up the group activities.
One creates a group, popularly known as a chama and invites friends either from his/her contacts or social media cycle to join. Also, one can join an already existing group that is short of members. People will be matched according to locality and interests to join/form groups. Each member is expected to contribute an agreed upon amount after a certain period and on the date of reimbursement the system will automatically reimburse the money to the recipients of that period as per the group guidelines that are set after all members approve them. The next recipient will receive the amount automatically on the next date of reimbursement. Each group will have a unique id that will be the group account number. When making payment to a common paybill number, the id (account number) will determine which account the money will go into.
